Game Modes

1. Free-For-All

Objective: Every player for themselves.

Rules: Each player tries to tag the others as many times as possible within a set time limit. The player with the most tags at the end wins.

2. Team Battle

Objective: Teams compete to get the most tags.

Rules: Split into two teams of two. The team with the most combined tags at the end of the game wins.

3. King of the Hill

Objective: Control a specific area of the map.

Rules: Designate a small area as the "hill." Players earn points by staying in the hill without being tagged. The player with the most time in the hill wins.

4. Capture the Flag

Objective: Capture the opposing team's flag and bring it back to your base.

Rules: Place flags in each team's base. Players must tag opponents to stop them from capturing their flag. If tagged while holding the flag, the player must drop it, and it resets to the original spot. First team to capture the flag wins.

5. Zombie Mode

Objective: Survive as long as possible.

Rules: One player starts as the zombie. When a zombie tags a human, the human becomes a zombie. The game continues until all players are zombies. The last human standing wins.

6. Elimination

Objective: Be the last player standing.

Rules: Each player has a limited number of lives. Once a player is tagged a set number of times, they are out of the game. The last player remaining wins.

7. Assassin

Objective: Secretly eliminate your assigned target.

Rules: Each player is given the name of another player as their target. They must tag their target while avoiding being tagged by their own assassin. If you tag your target, you get their target. The game continues until one player remains.

8. Protect the VIP

Objective: Protect the VIP player while the other team tries to tag them.

Rules: One team designates a VIP, and the other team tries to tag the VIP within a set time. If the VIP survives, their team wins; otherwise, the attacking team wins.

9. Domination

Objective: Control designated points on the map.

Rules: Place several control points around the map. Teams gain points by holding control points for a certain amount of time. The team with the most points at the end wins.

10. Rescue Mission

Objective: Rescue a "hostage" and bring them to your base.

Rules: One player is designated as the hostage and placed in a specific area. Teams compete to tag the hostage and escort them back to their base. The team that successfully rescues the hostage wins.
